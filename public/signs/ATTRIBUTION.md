# Swiss road sign assets

All SVGs in this directory are renderings of official Swiss road signs as
defined in the Signalisationsverordnung (**SR 741.21 — SSV**) of 5 September
1979 and its annexes.

## Licensing

Under the Swiss Federal Copyright Act (URG, **SR 231.1**) Article 5,
"official acts and documents of public authorities" are not protected by
copyright. The signs themselves — i.e. the geometric and typographic
designs codified by SSV — are therefore in the public domain in
Switzerland. The Wikimedia Commons rendering of each design is contributed
under one of:

- `{{PD-Switzerland-official}}` — explicit PD tag for SSV-defined signs.
- `{{PD-shape}}` — geometric shape too simple to attract copyright.
- `{{CC-BY-SA}}` — credited author release.

We re-distribute these renderings unmodified. For any sign whose Wikimedia
file page asserts a CC-BY-SA license rather than PD, we credit the
contributor in the per-file table below; otherwise the SSV / public-domain
basis applies.

## Source

All files were fetched from Wikimedia Commons via
`https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:FilePath/<original-filename>`
on the date of the most recent change to this directory (see `git log -p
public/signs/`). The `scripts/download-signs.ps1` script in this repo and
the manifest at `scripts/signs-manifest.tsv` document the exact mapping
between local code (e.g. `1.01.svg`) and Wikimedia filename (e.g.
`CH-Gefahrensignal-Linkskurve.svg`).

## Reproducibility

To re-run the download (e.g. to refresh a damaged file):

```powershell
powershell -NoProfile -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File scripts/download-signs.ps1
```

The script is idempotent — it skips files already present at ≥200 bytes.
